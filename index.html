Who we are

A thriving green community hub

Mohaduba Enterprise Nursery has experienced substantial growth since opening in Kuruman. We've built upon that growth to reach even more new customers — with creative marketing and a quality choice of plants for every garden.

Our goal is to be a stylish, respected and consistent garden centre that serves the Kuruman and surrounding areas. Every customer deserves a pleasant shopping experience guided by knowledgeable, caring staff.

Our purpose

Our Mission

Mohaduba Enterprise Nursery is dedicated to providing a wide variety of plants and trees in an aesthetic setting. Customer service is extremely important to us — we want each visitor to have a pleasant, memorable shopping experience.

It is the intention of our staff to answer questions with genuine expertise and to offer thoughtful advice when we feel it is needed. Every plant we sell carries our commitment to quality.

"Stylish, respected and consistent — a garden centre Kuruman can be proud of."
