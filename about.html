Our story

Rooted in Kuruman, grown with passion

Mohaduba Enterprise Nursery is not just a business — it is the lifelong dream of Joseph Sedumedi, realized and thriving. What started as a vision to bring a stylish, respected garden centre to the Kuruman area has blossomed into a flourishing nursery known for its knowledgeable staff and exceptional variety of plants.

For the past several years, the nursery has experienced substantial growth — reaching new customers, expanding greenhouse production, and building a reputation for creative marketing and quality garden supplies. Joseph personally manages every aspect of the operation, from ordering trees and merchandise to nurturing the state-of-the-art greenhouses.

Today, Mohaduba serves both residential garden lovers and professional landscaping contractors, offering a one-stop destination for plants, trees, and all the accessories a great garden needs.
