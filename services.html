At Mohaduba Enterprise Nursery, we cultivate a wide universe of plants and garden essentials — from bedding flowers to shade trees, and every garden tool in between. We take pride in what we grow and in the advice we give.

Most of our plants are grown on-site in state-of-the-art greenhouses, ensuring robust, healthy, acclimatised stock perfectly suited to the Kuruman climate. Whether you're a homeowner, landscaping contractor, or renter, we have the perfect green addition waiting for you.
